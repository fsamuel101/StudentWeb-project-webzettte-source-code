ABOUT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

College of Engineering History

The College of Engineering was first established as part of the Department of Technology and Engineering (DTE) since Academic year 2012 under the leadership of Dr. Sigfredo C. Adajar. The Department of Technology and Engineering is being divided into two colleges such as: College of Engineering and College of Computer Studies and Technology in Academic Year 2020-2021. Currently the department offers Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and is now under the leadership of Engr. Solita M. Costales.

College Goals

c. Deliver knowledge and skills of academic excellence towards life-long employability
